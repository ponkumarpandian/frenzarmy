ABOUT BADMINTON

POWERED BY

Badminton is a game which brings the birds of all flocks together. It creates a bond between people and creates a family comprising of folks of different age groups, gender, places, work culture, life styles, etc. We associate ourselves under Lev Pro Family, HSR Family, E-City Family, Sportive Family and what not.


While most of them spend time cribbing about office, personal life in the evenings and weekends or sit on a couch and regret thing or go to pubs just to forget life  for one evening, We live our life every day playing with our baddies


Building a unique and crazy group whom we call FrenzArmy. Which makes us feel lively here there and everywhere.

Most of us look forward to play our daily games where we get our Adrenaline Rush. It is an addiction, the good and healthy addiction.
